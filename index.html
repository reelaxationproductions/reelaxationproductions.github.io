Who are we?

Welcome to Reelaxation Productions, an innovative Australian film studio dedicated to crafting captivating short films, movies, and shows. What sets us apart? Our commitment to providing free access to our cinematic creations. Utilizing the cutting-edge capabilities of Unreal Engine for animation and Davinci Resolve for editing, our passionate production team labors tirelessly to deliver nothing short of excellence.

But our journey doesn't end there. We're more than just a studio; we're a community. Our dedicated team not only strives to produce remarkable content but also endeavors to build meaningful connections with our audience.

While you might have reservations initially, we assure you that your perception of us will transform. Why? Because your satisfaction is our priority, and we'll stop at nothing to exceed your expectations. After all, your opinion matters greatly to us. Welcome to a realm where storytelling thrives, and imagination knows no bounds. Welcome to Reelaxation Productions.
