Who are we?

Reelaxation Productions is an Australian film studio, we make shortfilms, movies & shows. The best part is we don't charge anything to watch our films. We animate in Unreal Engine and edit our films in Davinci Resolve, our dedicted production team works tirelessly to create the best possible films we can, and our wonderful community team gives a bridge between you and our team. You might think we are the worst studio ever but we can tell you one thing, we will work so increadably hard to change your mind because you matter to us!
